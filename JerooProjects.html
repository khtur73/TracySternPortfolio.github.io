Solution:

You need to make the Jeroo fill the outer square, then the inside square, and so on.

Here's directions step by step what U need to do:

  • Create new Jeroo and give it 575 flowers.
  • Create new method.
  • Inside the method, use command "While(hasFlowers())" - its means that the jeroo continue do the act untill it runs out of flowers.
  • Use "if(isClear(AHEAD))" inside the while command. then put inside "plant(); hop(); ". When the Jeroo sees that its clear ahead, he will put flower and hop.
  • Use "if(isWater(AHEAD))" inside the while command after the isclear command. then put inside "turn(RIGHT)". When the Jeroo sees water in front of it (once) it will turn right.
  • Use "if(isFlower(AHEAD))" inside the while command after the if isfacing water command. then put inside "turn(RIGHT)". When the Jeroo sees flower in front of it, it will turn right.
