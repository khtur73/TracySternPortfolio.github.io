Problem description:

Use the Jeroo to fill the space with flowers, without step on flowers, and make it to the middle when the screen full with flowers.

*Jeroo CAN'T step on flowers. The only flower it can step on is the flower that it puts in the middle at the end. He also can step on flower when it puts the flower.

*In the end the Jeroo needs to be in the middle, facing North, when all the screen covered by flowers.
